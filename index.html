Top Restaurants of Australia

1. QUAY

NSW,Sydney

Quay is a restaurant in Sydney, Australia. It is owned by Leon Fink, and is run by Chef Peter Gilmore. It has won several awards in Australia. Quay serves contemporary Australian cuisine.

2. ATTICA

Melbourne

Attica is a fine-dining restaurant in Melbourne, Australia. It has won several awards in Australia. Food type is Modern Australia Cuisine.

3. Momofuku Seiobo

NSW , Sydney

The Sydney based restaurant is the first venture for the Momofuku group outside of the U.S. and seamlessly blends Japanese kaiseki methods with western techniques.

4. Marque

NSW,Sydney

The menu is predominantly French influenced that delivers highly intelligent and original flavour combinations.

5. Vue de Monde

VIC,Melbourne

The name literally translates to 'view of the world' and represent's Bennett's imaginative and ambitious view of the world through food and wine.

6. Sepia

NSW,Sydney

Showcasing high quality Australian produce that is ethically and sustainably sourced, with a focus on seafood and beef, the menu contains many Asian influenced ingredients and textures.
